# Guidelines and Rules

## Calculating Multiple Discounts (Diskon Bertingkat)
When dealing with multiple discounts (e.g., "15% + 20%"):
1. **Calculate from the original price**: Each discount percentage is calculated based on the **original base price**, not from the remaining subtotal. (e.g., 15% of total + 20% of total = 35% of total).
2. **Keep the presentation separate**: When showing or listing the discounts, keep the units separate as specified by the user (e.g., "Diskon 15%" and "Diskon 20%") so that each party knows their respective discount portion.
3. **Total Discount**: The total discount is simply the sum of all the individual discount amounts.

Example: For Rp 33.000.000.000 with discount 15% + 20%:
- Initial Price: Rp 33.000.000.000
- Diskon 15%: Rp 4.950.000.000 (15% dari 33.000.000.000)
- Diskon 20%: Rp 6.600.000.000 (20% dari 33.000.000.000)
- Total Diskon: Rp 11.550.000.000
- Final Total: Rp 21.450.000.000
